Services Offered


Individual, Family, and Marriage Counseling is offered for issues including, but not limited to:

  • Depression and Anxiety
  • Marital Concerns
  • Relationship Concerns, including Family, Friends Children,Community
  • Adolescent Concerns
  • Career and Employment Concerns
  • Grief and Loss
  • Abuse
  • Stress
  • Parenting concerns
  • Child Academic and Behavioral Concerns
  • Anger Management
  • ADHD
  • Process Addictions, including Food, Sex, Money, Internet
  • Eating Disorders
  • Sexual Issues
  • PTSD (Post Traumatic Stress Disorder)
